My Hour of Code Map

By: Viviana

2 tablespoons of sugar 3 tablespoons of cocoa 1 teaspoon of crushed peppermint 20 marshmallows sprinkle ground cinnamon on top to add later: 2-3 cups of milk Instructions: pour ingredients into microwave-safe mug, then stir together, add milk, stir more, then put the mug into the microwave, and warm up for 1 minute, then let it cool. And then stir again, and then you're done.
