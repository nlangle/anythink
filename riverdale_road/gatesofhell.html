About Riverdale Road

Gates of Hell


Okay, so these gates are actually just made of rusty iron, but it’s the story behind them that adds them to almost every page on the internet about Colorado lore. People visit these gates when they’re seeking a thrill because of what or who they might see. The man who built them lost his mind when they finished, burning his entire mansion with his family asleep inside. He was never caught or put on trial. Now, witnesses report that a lady in white walks the road by the gates. Assumed to be his wife, she searches the street for her murdered children or even her husband. She might be out for revenge, but the most she’s ever done to visitors is appear in the rearview mirror as they pass by, longing for help that never comes.
