The Hitchiker


A doctor was driving home from the store and deiced to take the easier way home then taking his normal route. Whiling he's driving home, he notices a girl in a white gown standing on the side of the road. The doctor pulls over and asks if she need a ride home. She says yes and gets in the man's car. He asks for her address, she tells him the address. The doctor asks he why were you in the middle of nowhere. She stays quiet and just stares out the window, and the man asks again why were you in the middle of nowhere. Still no answer. When they get to the house he tells her that they made it to her house. He looks back and there is now one is sight. He goes outside and then knocks on the door of the house. An old man walks out and the doctor explains what happened. The man says this has been happing a lot lately. He tells him the his daughter is the person he "brought" with him. The doctor says that he must be wrong. Then the old man tells him to check the graveyard and look for a certain name. He goes to the graveyard and finds that grave of the girl he brought with him. It says she died 15 years ago in a car crash.
