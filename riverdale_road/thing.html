About The Thing


One dark night with no moon, a man and his friend were driving along Riverdale they were running late for a dinner with other friends. Suddenly, their car spluttered and died. The two friends got out of the car to check what was wrong, they couldn't see what was wrong, even with flashlights. So they decided to call somebody. One of the friends got out his phone but it was dead, so the other friend got out HIS phone but it was also dead. Then they heard a screech that chilled their bones. They froze the screech got closer and closer and closer, until it felt like whatever what was making the noise was right on top of them. Finally they leapt into the car. Whatever it was slammed into the car. the two friends screamed, then the thing materialized right in front of the hood. It was a creature so terrifying both men fainted dead away, the creature had peeling skin, and jagged teeth and nails, the creature screeched one last time and vanished. 20 years later in 2018 the same exact thing happened to a couple, luckily nobody died either time. The creature seems harmless, but who knows what the creature is planning to do?

This story told by: Viviana on 10/25/2018
