The Sad Wolpert Mansion


David Wolpert was a normal man, who led a normal life...

To an extent. He built a House, a normal everyday House. 9190 Riverdale Road. Oh, what a perfect family. David and Cathy Wolpert. David Jr., Lucille, and Mary.

Fifty years later, David Wolpert is dead. House is now Mansion. 1975. The chicken house was burned down. Mansion is declared bad things like "fire hazard" or "danger to the community". People decide to let Mansion decay.

The Wolperts are now dead, and Mansion is now sad. Coincidence? I think not. Oh, what a perfect family. David and Cathy Wolpert. David Jr., Lucille, Mary, Mansion, and all the lost souls who dig their noses too deep in the history of Wolpert Mansion. The ones who read this story. The ones like You.
